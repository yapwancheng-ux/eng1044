Project Milestones (13 Weeks)

Week 1: Assignment Briefing

Lecturer provided a comprehensive briefing on the coursework components, outlining the grading rubrics, submission deadlines, and academic integrity expectations.

Week 2: Group Forming & Pre-research

Finalized team members and established communication channels. Conducted preliminary research to identify viable topics related to AI in e-commerce.

Week 3: Find Portfolio Platform

Evaluated various platforms for the research portfolio. Selected a coding-based approach (HTML/CSS) to ensure customizability and adherence to technical requirements.

Week 4: Approval of Topic & Thesis Statement

Submitted the proposed research topic and thesis statement to the lecturer. Received approval to proceed with the specific focus on AI tools in start-ups.

Week 5: Gathering Sources & Constructing Outline

Collected academic journals and credible articles (post-2020). Developed a structured outline organizing main arguments and counter-arguments.

Week 6: Assignment Outlines Consultation

Presented the detailed research outline during consultation. Received feedback on the logical flow and strength of the proposed arguments.

Week 7: Individual Written Response

Drafted and finalized the Individual Written Response, focusing on personal synthesis of the initial research findings and theoretical concepts.

Week 8: Mid-Sem Break

Utilized the break to review feedback from the written response and deepen the literature review for the upcoming major research paper draft.

Week 9: Drafting Research Paper

Began the formal writing process for the research paper, expanding the outline into full paragraphs with proper APA in-text citations.

Week 10: Research Paper Consultation

Discussed the draft with the lecturer, focusing on critical evaluation of information, assessing reliability, credibility, and relevance of sources.

Week 11: Research Paper Editing and Proofreading

Conducted a rigorous peer-review session. Checked for grammatical errors, logical fallacies, and ensured strict adherence to APA 7th edition formatting.

Week 12: Final Review & Portfolio Polish

Finalized the digital portfolio layout, ensuring all artifacts were uploaded. Conducted a final walkthrough of the research paper to ensure zero plagiarism.

Week 13: Submission

Submitted the final Research Paper and the link to this Digital Research Portfolio via the student portal before the deadline.

Research Outline

3. Arguments

A. Enhanced Customer Experience (Personalization).

B. Operational Efficiency (Supply Chain).

4. Counterarguments & Conclusion

Addressing privacy concerns and high implementation costs. Summary of findings.
